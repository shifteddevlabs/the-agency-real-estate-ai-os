# Examples

## Example 1, New Buyer Text

Input:

```text
Buyer lead under 800k, moving in 90 days, likes East Austin, missing financing and must-haves.
```

Output:

```md
## Draft Status
Client-ready draft

## Channel
Text

## Draft
Hi Alex, thanks for reaching out. Happy to help you get oriented.

A few quick questions so I can point you in the right direction:
1. Are you already pre-approved or still choosing a lender?
2. What are your must-haves besides staying under 800k?
3. Is your 90-day move timing firm or flexible?

If useful, we can also do a quick 15-minute call and map out the best search plan.

## Human Review Notes
No neighborhood recommendation yet. Wait for criteria and financing status.
```
