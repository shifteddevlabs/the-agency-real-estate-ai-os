# Research Sources

This folder uses the source policy in `../../support/reference/research-sources.md`.

## Fast Research Checklist

For a property:

- Team-provided listing or MLS sheet.
- County appraisal district.
- City permit records.
- Floodplain or environmental maps if relevant.
- HOA, survey, title, or disclosure documents if provided.
- Recent comps from approved brokerage source.

For a neighborhood or market:

- Realtor.com Research Data Library.
- Redfin Data Center.
- FHFA House Price Index.
- U.S. Census API.
- HUD USPS ZIP Code Crosswalk API.
- City of Austin Open Data.
- OpenStreetMap or other approved geocoding, with attribution.

## Citation Format

```md
- Source:
- URL or internal document:
- Date checked:
- What it supports:
- Limits or warnings:
```

## Language Guardrails

Use:

- "The public data source shows..."
- "This suggests..."
- "This needs verification..."
- "The client may want to review..."

Avoid:

- "safe neighborhood"
- "bad area"
- "good for families"
- "up and coming because of demographics"
- "guaranteed appreciation"
- "definitely worth..."
