# Team Setup

Use this once before a real team uses The Agency.

The goal is simple: tell the AI who does what, where the official records live, and when a human must review before anything moves forward.

Do not paste passwords, private client details, wire instructions, or full transaction files into this setup note.

## People

- Who is on the team?
- What does each person handle?
- Who can approve a client message without Diana?
- Who covers for Diana when she is unavailable?
- Who is the broker or compliance reviewer?
- Who watches new leads after hours?
- Who owns transaction deadlines?

## Official Records

- Where do new leads arrive?
- Where does the team keep the CRM record?
- Where are signed contracts stored?
- Where are inspection reports, disclosures, title documents, and HOA documents stored?
- Where should research notes go?
- What should never be copied into an AI chat?

## Leads

- Which lead sources are active, referrals, website, Zillow, open houses, social, phone, email?
- What response time does the team promise?
- What information is required before a lead is active?
- What should happen if the lead already has an agent?
- What makes a lead a poor fit?
- How should the team handle a budget that does not match the client's expectations?

## Research

- Which MLS exports or brokerage reports are approved?
- Which county, city, school, floodplain, permit, HOA, tax, and title sources are approved?
- How old can a source be before it must be checked again?
- Which paid data providers are allowed?
- Who decides what to do when two sources disagree?

## Diana's Voice

Run [voice-onboarding.md](voice-onboarding.md).

Then decide:

- When should the message sound like Diana personally?
- When should it sound like a neutral team update?
- How long should texts usually be?
- How long should emails usually be?
- What words should never appear in client messages?
- How should tone change for referrals, past clients, investors, first-time buyers, sellers, or vendors?

## When To Ask A Human

Use this default list until Diana customizes it.

| Issue | Example | Ask |
|---|---|---|
| Fair housing | "Is this area good for families?" | Diana or broker |
| Advertising | Listing captions, social posts, website copy | Broker |
| Legal | Contract rights, disputes, default, termination | Attorney or broker |
| Lending | Approval, rates, payment estimates | Lender |
| Tax | Capital gains, deductions, investment tax treatment | CPA or tax advisor |
| Appraisal | Value guarantee, appraisal outcome | Appraiser or lender |
| Inspection | Repair severity, safety, lifespan, cost | Inspector or specialist |
| Insurance | Flood, hazard, premiums, insurability | Insurance professional |
| Wire instructions | Any account or routing detail | Title or escrow by verified phone call |
| Privacy | Personal hardship, finances, family details | Diana, share as little as possible |

## Active Deals

- What happens right after an accepted offer?
- What happens after inspection?
- What happens when appraisal is ordered?
- What happens when title commitment arrives?
- What happens before the final walk-through?
- What happens during closing week?
- What proof is required before a task is marked done?
- What is the fastest same-day channel for urgent issues?

## Ready For A Pilot

The team is ready to test The Agency when:

- roles are filled in
- official record locations are known
- Diana's voice profile is approved
- human-review rules are customized
- the transaction record system is named
- five mock scenarios pass with human review
